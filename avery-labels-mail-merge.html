Frequently Asked Questions

Can you make Avery® labels in Google Docs?

To print Avery® labels in Google Docs, install Labelmaker and open Google Docs. Launch Labelmaker from the Add-ons menu to start designing your labels.

Can you make Avery® labels in Google Sheets?

To print Avery® labels in Google Sheets, install Labelmaker and open Google Sheets. Launch Labelmaker from the Add-ons menu to start designing your labels.

Does Google Docs have Avery® templates?

There are 1500 Avery® Templates for Google Docs available in the Avery® Mail Merge compatible add-on by Labelmaker.

How do I put my logo on Avery® labels?

You can put your logo on Avery® labels by inserting images in your template. Learn how to insert a picture or a logo into an Avery® Label.

Can you make Avery® labels from an Excel spreadsheet?

It’s possible to make Avery® labels from an Excel spreadsheet. Either use the built-in mail merge tool in Microsoft Office or import your Excel file to your Google Drive. Then launch Labelmaker to create Avery® Labels from Excel.
